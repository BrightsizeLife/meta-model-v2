# Raw Data

This directory contains raw, unprocessed data files.

**Purpose:** Store original datasets before any transformations or cleaning.

## Files

- `*_games.csv` - NFL game schedules and scores by season (2018-2025)
- `game_stats_2022_w12-w18.csv` - Offensive and defensive team stats for 2022 weeks 12-18 (108 games, 77 columns)
- `game_stats_2023.csv` - Complete offensive and defensive team stats for 2023 season (286 games, 77 columns per schema)
- `game_stats_2024.csv` - Complete offensive and defensive team stats for 2024 season (286 games, 77 columns per schema)
- `game_stats_2025.csv` - Complete offensive and defensive team stats for 2025 season (286 games, 77 columns per schema)
